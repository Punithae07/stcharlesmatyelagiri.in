About US

ST.CHARLES MATRIC SCHOOL, ATHANAVUR was established in 1975 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in JOLARPET block of VELLORE district of Tamil Nadu. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 11. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Non-Ashram type (Govt.) in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

Principal’s Message

As per the ancient Indian culture, education means walking with God. Education leads to removal of darkness as we trot along good. Education is an everlasting light to enlighten our minds and hearts. This school has a great vision to provide quality education and create citizens who will be known for their love of God, love of neighbor, and love of society, the nation, and the world at large.
